EPISODES

Relive the journey from the beginning. Navigate
through seasons to uncover the truth.

THE PLOT

A small town, a dark secret, and a dimensional
tear that changes everything.

SEASON 1
When a young boy vanishes, a small town uncovers a mystery involving secret experiments, terrifying supernatural forces, and one strange little girl.
SEASON 2
It's 1984 and the citizens of Hawkins are still reeling from the horrors of the Demogorgon. A bigger, sinister entity threatens those who survived.
SEASON 3
Summer of 1985 brings a new mall, romance, and new enemies. As the Mind Flayer finds a new host, everyone must band together to survive.
SEASON 4
Separated across the globe, our heroes face Vecna, a terrifying new threat from the Upside Down whose gruesome curse shakes the foundation of Hawkins.
SEASON 5
The ultimate battle approaches. As the Upside Down bleeds into Hawkins in unprecedented ways, the final stand against the darkness begins.
